Phase 31 Plan

Phase 31 will be about creating a new mod, RingOfCreation

References:
Level13PlusGear/ - Small mod that should be the basis of this new mod
ExampleMods/OldMagicRingMod/ - a mod that creates rings
ExampleMode/cheatersring_2d1ca631-35dd-3af-bpmv/ - a mod with a lot of spells that summon items

The goal is to create a new ring, the "Ring of Creation".
The ring will have the ability to summon items by ID. I'm not sure which id, but that is probably handled in the cheatersring mod.
I think the cheatersring mod actually creates scrolls that cast spells that summon items.

Ideally, the Ring of Creation will summon the Level 13 plus gear chest. (from the Level13PlusGear mod)

But to verify things work, I also want it to summon a potion of supreme healing:
	<MapKey>7d78f227-e8d4-486d-8121-25cf0bee751d</MapKey>
	<Name>CONS_Potion_Healing_A_Supreme</Name>

And, as another test, summon a pair of Boots of the Stormwalker, which we made for SampleMagicRingMod.

Yes, this mod will depend on the Level13PlusGear and SampleMagicRingMod mods.

The Level13PlusGear mod is a good mod to use as a basis for this new RingOfCreation mod, because the Level13PlusGear mod is new and small.

We should have a copy of the Ring of Creation placed in both the tutorial chest and the traveler's chest.
